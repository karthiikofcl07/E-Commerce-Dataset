# Brazilian E-Commerce Business Insights & Executive Report

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive end-to-end data analysis of the Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset (Olist).  
The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights that support strategic decision-making.

The analysis focuses on revenue performance, regional sales distribution, product category trends, customer behavior, and growth patterns.

---

## Objectives

• Analyze overall revenue performance  
• Identify top-performing regions and product categories  
• Evaluate monthly revenue trends and growth rates  
• Examine order volume and average order value (AOV)  
• Generate actionable business recommendations

---

## Dataset Source

Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist  
Available on Kaggle

The dataset consists of multiple relational CSV files including:

- Orders
- Order Items
- Products
- Customers
- Payments
- Reviews

## Key Insights

• Revenue exceeded $14M with consistent growth trends  
• Sales are concentrated in major metropolitan regions  
• Top product categories drive a significant share of total revenue  
• Seasonal revenue patterns were identified  
• Order volume trends align closely with revenue performance
